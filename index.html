Our Process

01.

Brief

Our website process begins with identifying the requirements and goals. We conduct a critical review and research, which helps our designer and content strategist align the project. The project manager then creates wireframes.

02.

Design

Our creative team develops the design while keeping up with the latest trends. After several review rounds, the approved design is handed over to the development team for implementation.

03.

Development

Our developers start building the website on a secure server. You’ll receive a private URL to test your site before it goes live.

04.

Finalization & Deployment

We conduct thorough testing on beta servers before deploying the site. Even after launch, we offer support to ensure everything runs smoothly.
